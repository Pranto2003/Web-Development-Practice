OM Bhur bhuva sva Tat saviturvarenya bhargo devasya dhimahi. Dhiyo yo na prachodayat.

With the knowledge of Being, Becoming, and Spirit, with knowledge, karma and prayer, we meditate upon the blazing glory of self-effulgent lord Savita, Lord of existence, intelligence and bliss, the only worthy choice of ours, and we pray that He may inspire and guide our vision and intelligence to the right path. YajurVeda - 36/3
